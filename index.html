Crystal Game

Here are your instructions, fam:


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are 4 crystals. When you click on a crystal, you add a specfic amount of points to the total score.


To win the game, have your total score match the random number. If your score goes above the random number, you lose.


You will not know the value of each crystal until you click on it.


Every time a new game starts, the value of the crystals will change. Good luck!
